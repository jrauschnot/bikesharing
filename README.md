# Bikesharing Analysis

# Project Overview:
An analysis of bike-sharing data from New York City using Tableau and Pandas to determine the liklihood of success of a similar venture in Des Moines, IA..

## Resources:

* Data Sources:
    - 201908-citibike-tripdata.csv
    - NYC_CitiBike_Challenge.ipynb

* Software: Tableau, Jupyter Notebooks, Pandas

## Results:

1. Length of Checkout Time: 

<img width="784" alt="Length_of_checkout_Time" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93015602/159176122-ddadee8e-a05b-45e9-b32c-385211e54361.png">

A visualization of the length of checkout time per ride.  The majority of rides fall less than 1 hour in total time while the peak ride time is only approximately 5 min in duration. 

2. Length of Checkout Time by Gender:

<img width="884" alt="Length_of_checkout_time_bygender" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93015602/159176121-6f92be29-69cf-45bd-aaf0-bb7ba8dbf9a4.png">

A visualization of the length of checkout time per ride by gender.  Males are depicted in orange, Females in blue, and Unknown in green. As can be observerd, the Males account for the majority of rides.  

3. Trips by Weekday for Each Hour:

![Trips by Weekday for Each Hour](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93015602/159176075-21903e96-abd6-4d5b-8749-7efad464e351.png)

A visualization of trips by hour as shown by a heat map.  Darker colors represent higher volume of rides.  The data shows that rides are most common between 0700-0900 & 1700-1900 suggesting a relationship to the normal workday hours. 

4. Trips by Gender (Weekday per Hour):

![Trips by Gender (Weekday per Hour)](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93015602/159176072-0e7ea563-8180-44ec-9e10-88180f8e6c2e.png)

A visualization of trips by hour and gender.  Darker colors represent higher volume of rides.  The data shows that rides are most common between 0700-0900 & 1700-1900 suggesting a relationship to the normal workday hours. More specifically, the male rides account for the majority of rides. 

5. Trips by Gender by Weekday: 

![Trips by Gender by Weekday](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93015602/159176074-a5c4ca7e-7932-4cc7-ba94-06280fde13e4.png)

A visualization of trips by Gender and Weekday. The majortiy of rides for both Males and Females are by subscription users and not customers.
